# Project Brief

## Goal

Create a modern dentist website for Smile Dental Care that builds trust, introduces the doctor and team, showcases the clinic, and encourages visitors to call or contact the practice.

- Primary doctor shown in assets: Dr. Bassam Ali

## Suggested Pages

- Home
- About the Doctor
- Meet the Team
- Services
- New Patients
- Gallery
- Contact

## Missing Details To Collect

- Email
- Hours
- Services offered
- Insurance and payment details
- Appointment booking URL
- Patient testimonials or reviews
- Compliance or privacy requirements

## Visual Direction

Use the real team and clinic photography as the foundation. Aim for a clean, bright, modern healthcare look with high contrast, accessible typography, generous whitespace, and clear calls to action.
