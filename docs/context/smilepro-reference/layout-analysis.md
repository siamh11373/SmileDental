# Layout Analysis

## Header And Navigation

The desktop header has two layers. The top utility bar is dark charcoal with "Accepting New Patients" on the left, address in the middle, phone number, and a gold "Book Now" button on the right. The main navigation underneath is a white or warm-white bar with a small centered logo and uppercase navigation links around it.

Desktop navigation links:

- About with dropdown.
- Invisalign with dropdown.
- Offers.
- Services with dropdown.
- Appointment.
- Contact.
- Blog.

The iPad portrait layout keeps the dark utility bar, but the main navigation becomes a centered white floating bar with the logo and compact hamburger/phone icons.

The mobile layout hides the top utility bar and uses a large white header with logo on the left, hamburger in the upper-right area, and a phone icon at the far right.

## Hero

The hero is dark, full-width, and high contrast. It uses a blurred team photo or clinic photo as the background with a charcoal overlay. Desktop places the headline and CTAs on the left and a large team photo on the right. iPad portrait keeps the dark background but stacks the large team image below the main text. Mobile stacks everything vertically: logo header, headline, large team image, supporting copy, then large pill CTAs.

Hero elements:

- Serif headline: "Exceptional Dental Care" in the reference.
- Small supporting line about financing.
- Gold filled "Book Now" CTA.
- Gold filled or matching "Call Now" CTA.
- Google rating row with stars.
- ADA/accreditation badges near the lower hero area on desktop.

For Smile Dental, use the wide team/building image or interior team photo for the hero image. Use a dark overlay so white type remains readable.

## Services Grid

The services section sits on a light cream background. It starts with a left-aligned serif heading and a small outlined pill button on the right. The service cards are image tiles with white bottom labels and thin spacing between cards.

Desktop uses a three-column by two-row grid. iPad portrait uses two columns. Mobile uses a single column with full-width cards.

Reference services:

- Preventative.
- Crowns & Bridges.
- Endodontics.
- Dental Implants.
- Invisalign.
- Colored Bonding.

For Smile Dental, keep the same visual card pattern even if service names change later.

## Featured Treatment Sections

After the services grid, the reference repeats large alternating treatment blocks. Each block has one text column and one image column. The image column often has a thin gold rectangular border offset behind the image.

Section backgrounds alternate between pale blush and near-white/cream. Spacing is generous. Headings are large serif text. Body copy is compact, with short paragraphs or bullets. Each section ends with a small outlined "Read More" pill.

Reference order:

- Invisalign Clear Aligners: text left, image right.
- Dental Implants: image left, text right.
- Cosmetic Dentistry: text left, image right.
- Restorative Dentistry: image left, text right.
- Preventive Care: text left, image right.
- Traditional Orthodontics: image left, text right.

Use thin horizontal gold divider lines with a centered gold medallion between major groups.

## Why Choose Section

The "Why Choose" section is centered and calm. It uses a serif heading followed by a white card panel containing four narrow columns. Columns are separated by vertical charcoal lines on desktop.

Reference benefits:

- Comprehensive Care Under One Roof.
- State-of-the-Art Technology.
- Comfortable Environment.
- Flexible Financing Options.

Mobile should stack these cards vertically, keeping icon, title, and body centered.

## Appointment And Testimonials

The appointment section switches back to dark charcoal. The top of this section has a large serif CTA headline on the left and a cropped smiling-mouth image on the right. Under it sits a testimonial carousel style area.

Desktop shows three testimonial cards horizontally. The center card is emphasized with a border and star rating. Small gold carousel controls sit between cards. Mobile stacks the testimonial content and should support swipe or simple next/previous controls.

Keep review cards dark with white text, gold stars, and thin gold/cream borders.

## Team Section

The team section uses a warm cream-to-blush background. It has a centered serif heading and a grid of individual provider cards.

Desktop layout in the reference:

- First row: three cards centered.
- Second row: four cards.
- Each card has a portrait area, white lower label block, provider name, role, and small gold underline.
- A small outlined "About Us" pill sits below the grid.

For Smile Dental, use the doctor portrait first and add placeholders or crop team photos until individual portraits are supplied.

## Insurance Section

This section returns to pale blush. It uses a two-column layout with copy on the left and an image on the right, again with an offset gold image border. The heading is serif and prominent.

The copy reassures patients about insurance/payment help and has a small "Read More" outline button.

## Visit, Contact, And Footer

The contact region includes a thin text strip, a "Visit Us" heading, and a wide embedded map. Below the map is a warm beige footer area with office hours, contact details, quick links, service links, and a "Let's get you scheduled" CTA area.

For Smile Dental, keep the map block as a placeholder until the verified address is known.
